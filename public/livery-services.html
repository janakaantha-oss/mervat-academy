Stable Standards & Regulations

  • Vaccination Records — All horses accepted for livery must have up-to-date vaccination records, including Equine Influenza, which is required for competition under UAE Equestrian Federation and FEI regulations. Tetanus vaccination is strongly recommended for every horse in our care.
  • Owner Responsibility — It remains the horse owner's responsibility to ensure their horse's vaccination schedule stays current. Our team will request a copy of your horse's vaccination record upon arrival.
  • Security & Welfare — Our stable yard operates 24-hour security with CCTV coverage, consistent with standards followed by leading UAE equestrian facilities.
  • Climate Care — Given Dubai's summer temperatures, our facilities follow recommended equine welfare guidance for ventilation and cooling between June and September.
  • Veterinary & Farrier Access — Routine veterinary and farrier visits are coordinated as part of your horse's care. Any additional costs beyond routine care are billed separately.
  • Documentation — New horses joining our livery must provide proof of ownership and any relevant health certificates prior to arrival.
