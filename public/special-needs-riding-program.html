About the Package

Our Special Needs Riding Program offers a safe, supportive, and enjoyable riding experience tailored to individual needs. Sessions are led by trained instructors experienced in adaptive riding, focused on comfort, safety, and connection with the horse.

What's Included

  • Sessions led by instructors experienced in adaptive riding
  • Calm, well-suited school horses
  • Safety equipment and additional support as needed
  • A patient, encouraging environment

Membership Terms

5 Sessions Package

  • Valid for 2 months from the date of purchase.
  • Expires automatically after the validity period.
  • Sessions may be rescheduled with prior notice.

Who Is This Package For?

  • Riders with physical, sensory, or developmental needs
  • Riders who benefit from a calmer, shorter session format
  • All ages welcome
