About the Package

Ride and learn together in a small-group setting, supervised by our certified coaches. Group lessons are a great way to build confidence, learn from others, and enjoy the social side of riding while still receiving expert guidance.

What's Included

  • Small-group instruction with certified coaches
  • Use of a school horse for the lesson
  • Structured lesson plans suited to group skill level
  • Safety equipment provided during lessons
